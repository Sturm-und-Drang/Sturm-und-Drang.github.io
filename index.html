GEOMAGNETIC STORMS MAY 2024




The solar storms of May 2024 were a series of intense solar events caused by powerful solar flares and significant magnetic activity on celestial objects.
These natural events occurring beyond our protective atmosphere and magnetic field can affect human activities both on Earth and in space. Solar storm are known to cause disturbances in our magnetic field leading to satellite and communication disruptions, fluctuation, failures of power grids and can even influence human health.
However, the interaction of solar particles with the Earth's magnetic field also causes stunning aurorae, but they are not just vibrant colors, they are an active testament to the secrets of the intricate laws of our universe.
